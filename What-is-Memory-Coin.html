MemoryCoin (MMC)

A GPU/ASIC resistant cryptocurrency

What is MemoryCoin?

Memorycoin (MMC) is a cryptocurrency that uses a special Proof Of Work mechanism to prevent GPU/ASIC mining. The coin uses a small SHA512 component, like Bitcoin, but also uses AES in its proof of work mechanism. This design is chosen to make desktop mining competitive, thus providing a network with lots of small miners. This is beneficial for the stability and safety of the coin. Furthermore it has a relatively fast block time for fast transaction confirmation.

Another interesting innovation in MemoryCoin is the availability of a voting mechanism. This makes it possible to vote for leaders and developers of the coin. There are five positions available that get payed a share for their work. All stakeholders can stand as a candidate for the positions or vote on the candidates for each position. This makes the Memory Coin DAC democratic and autonomous. More information on the voting is available here.

All stakeholders in Protoshares can claim MemoryCoins. ProtoShares owners collectively received a 1% stake in the start of the blockchain. If you had Protoshares (PTS) on an address you controlled on block 32,000 you can use this guide to claim your Memorycoins!
